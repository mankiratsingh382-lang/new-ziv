About ZIVARR

Luxury jewellery, crafted to feel personal — and designed to last.

ZIVARR is a modern luxury brand inspired by Indian craftsmanship and shaped by contemporary femininity. We create sterling silver and gold plated pieces with a focus on comfort, refined shine, and heirloom-level detailing.

This is jewellery that moves with you—day to night, celebrations and quiet moments—made to be worn beautifully, again and again.
