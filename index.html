Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Designed for Today

"We believe jewellery is not decoration — it is memory, identity, and love made tangible."

Zivarr was born from a simple belief: that every Indian woman deserves jewellery that honours her roots while celebrating who she is becoming. Founded in 2019, we work with third-generation artisans across Rajasthan and Jaipur, combining age-old silversmithing techniques with contemporary design language.

Each piece passes through twelve hands before it reaches yours — designed, cast, polished, set, and finished with a care that machines simply cannot replicate.

Our Craft

The Art of Making

From first sketch to your doorstep — a journey of precision, passion, and craftsmanship.

01
Design

Each collection begins with hand-drawn sketches inspired by Indian motifs, temple architecture, and nature.

02
Casting

Lost-wax casting technique — a method over 4,000 years old — gives form to each intricate design with remarkable fidelity.

03
Setting

Each stone is hand-set under magnification by artisans who have spent decades perfecting the art of precision stone placement.

04
Quality Check

Every piece passes 24-point quality inspection and BIS hallmarking before being lovingly packaged for you.
