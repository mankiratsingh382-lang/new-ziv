Policies

Shipping, returns, and privacy guidance for every order.

This page consolidates the policy details expected in a premium shopping environment and keeps the tone consistent with the brand. It can be expanded into full policy documents as the store grows.

Shipping

Complimentary delivery is available on orders above ₹2,000. Standard delivery is 3–5 business days and express service is available for urgent pick-ups.

Returns

Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery for unused pieces in original packaging. Custom or engraved items are final sale.

Privacy

Your personal details are used solely for order fulfilment and account support. The backend API can be extended to include customer consent controls and data export tools.
